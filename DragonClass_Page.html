Hydro

The Hydro Class offers students the opportunity to dive deep into the mystical world of water. At Zen dragoN Academy, this class focuses on mastering the fluid and adaptive nature of water, allowing students to form strong bonds with water dragons. Training includes extensive studies on water manipulation, healing techniques, and strategic uses of water in various scenarios. Students in the Hydro Class develop into versatile and resourceful partners to their aquatic dragon counterparts, ensuring they can thrive in any environment.

Anemo

Zen dragoN Academy’s Anemo Class is dedicated to the study of wind and air. Students in this class learn to harness the power of the skies, developing agility, speed, and precision. The curriculum includes training in wind manipulation, aerodynamics, and aerial combat tactics, preparing students to collaborate seamlessly with wind dragons. By the end of their training, Anemo Class students are adept at using the wind to their advantage, capable of swift and strategic maneuvers alongside their dragon partners.

Pyro

The Pyro Class at Zen dragoN Academy is designed for students with a passion for the element of fire. This class teaches students to harness the power of flames, igniting their potential to become formidable allies to fire dragons. Through rigorous training and a deep understanding of fire’s characteristics, students learn to control and collaborate with fire dragons, using their skills for both combat and creative purposes. Graduates of the Pyro Class emerge as skilled flame-wielders, ready to face any fiery challenge.

Photon

The Photon Class at Zen dragoN Academy focuses on the radiant and enlightening element of light. This class trains students to use light in various forms, from illumination and healing to creating blinding flashes in battle. Students learn to work in harmony with light dragons, developing skills that enhance their strategic thinking and creative problem-solving. By mastering the properties of light, Photon Class graduates become beacons of hope and innovation, ready to illuminate any darkness with their dragon allies.

Geo

In the Geo Class, students immerse themselves in the strength and stability of the earth element. Zen dragoN Academy equips these students with the knowledge and skills to manipulate rocks, soil, and minerals, fostering a deep connection with earth dragons. Training covers a wide range of techniques, from defensive earth barriers to offensive seismic attacks. Graduates from the Geo Class are known for their resilience and fortitude, standing as steadfast protectors and powerful allies to their earth-bound dragon companions.
