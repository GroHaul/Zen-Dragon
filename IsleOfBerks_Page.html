History of Zen DragoN Academy : Forging Warriors of Dragonkind on the Isle of Berk

In 1995, on the Isle of Berk, Master Liang Chen, a revered dragon trainer and philosopher, set forth on a quest to establish a bastion of enlightenment and martial excellence. Recognizing the intricate bond between humans and dragons, Master Chen envisioned an institution that would transcend mere practicality, delving into the depths of wisdom and philosophy to foster a profound understanding of these mythical beings.

Thus, Zen DragoN Academy was born, its inception marking a pivotal moment in Berk's history. Under Master Chen's guidance, the academy became a sanctuary where aspiring riders not only honed their skills in dragon training but also delved into the profound principles that underpin the bond between rider and dragon.

Today, as the sun sets upon Berk, Zen DragoN Academy stands as a testament to the fusion of visionaries. Master Liang Chen's legacy endures, forever intertwined with the enduring bond between humans and dragons, and the noble pursuit of harmony and martial excellence.
